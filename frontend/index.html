The problem

Most African SMEs operate without any security monitoring or visibility. Security is only addressed after something goes wrong.

  • SMEs don't monitor their security infrastructure on an ongoing basis.
  • Security issues are discovered only after incidents occur, often by customers or attackers.
  • There's no clear ownership or understanding of security posture within the organization.

What Cyndro does

Cyndro provides continuous visibility into basic security fundamentals through a simple dashboard.

Central Dashboard

One place to see your security status at a glance with a simple risk score.

Website Monitoring

Track uptime, SSL certificates, and security headers continuously.

Timely Alerts

Get notified when security issues are detected so you're aware of your current status.

Regular Reports

Weekly and monthly summaries of your security posture sent directly to you.

What Cyndro does not do

Current scope

  • Cyndro is not a penetration testing service.
  • Cyndro does not provide full security protection or prevention.
  • Cyndro does not offer advanced threat detection or incident response.

Cyndro focuses on visibility and monitoring to help SMEs understand their security posture. It's a starting point, not a complete solution.

Who it's for

African SMEs with 10 to 100 employees who don't have a dedicated security team.

Organizations that need to start taking security seriously but don't know where to begin.

Businesses that want basic visibility into their security posture without overwhelming complexity.
